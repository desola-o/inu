About Us

Who we are

INU Legal Consulting is a Toronto-based immigration law firm dedicated to making the immigration process clear, accessible, and effective for individuals and families.

Our team provides trusted legal guidance across a wide range of immigration matters, including temporary and permanent residence applications, citizenship applications, and complex immigration appeals. Every matter is approached with care, precision, and a commitment to achieving the best possible outcome.

Contact INU Legal Consulting today to find out how we can help you at info@inulegalconsulting.ca
